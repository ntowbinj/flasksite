bio

Formed in Little Rock, AR in Fall of 2013, Foliage explores the combination of free and modal jazz improvisation and harmony with the rhythmic textures and intensity of metal, drum 'n bass, and hip-hop. All material is composed or arranged by the band. The majority is original, along with some substantially transformed covers of songs by acts like Stereolab and Boards of Canada

Foliage also plays occasionally as an acoustic jazz quartet, featuring their own energetic take on traditional standards
